Why Decidueye?

  • Moveset: Spirit Shackle & Shadow Sneak
  • Style: Ranged , Ranged Attacker / Supporter
  • Area: Central (main) / Bottom Path

Decidueye has been my main in Pokemon Unite for years. Before I settled with Decidueye, I used to main Lucario, Absol, and Zeraora. I finally ended up with Decidueye until now, because Decidueye fits my playing style the most. Not to mention, Decidueye is a ranged attacker, which I'm more comfortable with than close-ranged attackers. I'm also most familiar with Decidueye as well as the tactics to play it. Other than that, I just enjoy playing decidueye a lot
